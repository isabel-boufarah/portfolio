To Compete

Front End Design & Development

To Compete is a scroll based experience meant to describe what it means to compete as an athlete. From setting goals to celebrating post race, I hope to relay the emotional highs and consistent training I share with my teammates.

Collegiate Athlete Injury Experience

UX Research Case Study

As a college athlete, I have experienced myself and witnessed my teammates suffer through long injuries, battling with themselves and the support around them, trying to reach the finish line. As a junior I was well versed in this problem and decided it needed a solution. I tasked myself with understanding the needs, goals and pain points of injured athletes in order to improve the injury experience as a student athlete.
